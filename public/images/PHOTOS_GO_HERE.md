# How to add photos

## Hero cover photo
Drop a portrait photo of the couple here and name it:
  couple.jpg

It will appear automatically in the hero section.

## Gallery photos
Drop photos into the `gallery/` folder and name them:
  gallery/photo-01.jpg
  gallery/photo-02.jpg
  ... etc.

Then update `src/gallery-config.ts` to add titles, years, and captions.

Supported formats: .jpg .jpeg .png .webp
